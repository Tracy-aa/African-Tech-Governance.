Policy Analysis: Digital Sovereignty and AI Governance in Africa
Context: This sample was developed as part of my successful shortlisting for the 2026 ILINA Seminar, focusing on the intersection of emerging technology and African legal frameworks.

Key Themes:

Algorithmic Accountability: Analyzing the 'protection gap' for African digital entrepreneurs.

Data Sovereignty: Evaluating how local datasets should be protected under generative AI models.

Regulatory Innovation: Moving beyond 'copy-paste' legislation to create commercially viable tech policy for the Global South.

Objective: To demonstrate a balanced approach to tech regulation that prioritizes both innovation-enabling environments and robust consumer protection.
